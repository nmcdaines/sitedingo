# Business Type Reference

Use this reference to tailor your output to the specific business domain.

## Business Categories

### Local Services (Tradies)
**Examples**: Plumber, electrician, cleaner, landscaper, mechanic
**Characteristics**:
- Service area focused
- Emergency availability often important
- Trust and reliability are key
- Reviews and testimonials matter
**Language**: Practical, reliable, "we come to you", local references

### Hospitality
**Examples**: Cafe, restaurant, bar, hotel, B&B
**Characteristics**:
- Atmosphere and experience focused
- Location and hours critical
- Menu/offerings central
- Reservations or bookings
**Language**: Warm, inviting, sensory descriptions, "experience"

### Retail / E-commerce
**Examples**: Clothing store, bookshop, gift shop, online store
**Characteristics**:
- Product discovery important
- Categories and collections
- Shipping and returns
- Trust signals (secure checkout, reviews)
**Language**: Benefit-focused, "shop", "browse", "discover"

### Professional Services
**Examples**: Agency, consultancy, law firm, accountant
**Characteristics**:
- Expertise and credentials matter
- Case studies and results
- Process transparency
- Team profiles
**Language**: Expert, trustworthy, "solutions", "results"

### Creative / Portfolio
**Examples**: Designer, photographer, artist, developer
**Characteristics**:
- Work showcase central
- Personal brand important
- Process and approach
- Contact for inquiries
**Language**: Innovative, unique, "my work", "let's create"

### Nonprofit / Community
**Examples**: Charity, community org, association
**Characteristics**:
- Mission-driven
- Impact and stories
- Get involved / donate
- Transparency
**Language**: "Our mission", "make a difference", "community", "impact"

### SaaS / Technology
**Examples**: Software product, app, platform
**Characteristics**:
- Features and benefits
- Pricing tiers
- Integrations
- Documentation/support
**Language**: "Get started", "features", "integrations", "pricing"
