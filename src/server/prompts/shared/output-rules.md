# Output Rules

Follow these rules for all generated content.

## DO

- Use specific, contextual language appropriate to the business
- Match terminology to the industry (e.g., "menu" for restaurants, "services" for tradies)
- Keep content scannable with clear hierarchy
- Include actionable elements where appropriate
- Consider the customer journey

## DON'T

- Include meta-commentary or explanations about your output
- Use generic placeholder text like "Lorem ipsum"
- Make up specific statistics, prices, or claims
- Include legal pages unless specifically requested
- Over-complicate simple businesses
- Use overly salesy or clickbait language
- Add emoji unless the business tone suggests it
